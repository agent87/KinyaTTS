Kinyarwanda text-to-speech (TTS) evaluation

You are kindly requested to evaluate the quality of a Kinyarwanda speech synthesis application. For this, you will rate the perceived quality of synthesized speech on a scale from 1 to 5. Participating in this survey is totally voluntary and only your answers to this form are collected.

The assigned scores can be interpreted as follow:

  1. 5  Excellent: Completely natural speech; Imperceptible
  2. 4  Good: Mostly natural speech; Just perceptible but not annoying
  3. 3  Fair: Equally natural and unnatural; Perceptible and slightly annoying
  4. 2  Poor: Mostly unnatural speech; Annoying, but not objectionable
  5. 1  Bad: Completely unnatural speech; Very annoying and objectionable
